(About me)

Off the track, I'm Max Verstappen, but on it, I'm all about pushing limits and chasing wins. I began racing almost as soon as I could walk, following in the footsteps of my racing driver parents. Since then, I've dedicated myself to the thrill of Formula One, becoming the youngest ever competitor in the sport at just 17. Fueled by a competitive spirit and a natural talent for speed, I've pushed my limits and broken records. I secured my first win at the age of 18, becoming the youngest ever race winner in Formula One history. But that was just the beginning. In 2021, I achieved my dream, becoming the first ever Dutch Formula One World Champion. I went on to claim back-to-back championships in 2022 and 2023.
